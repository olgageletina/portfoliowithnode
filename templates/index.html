About

I'm a product designer and educator. Currently at The Hartford Innovation Lab, my responsibilities span the whole product lifecycle — from sketching to creating the final prototypes. I also lead the handoff process by partnering with engineering on front-end UI, developing our design system, and gathering data on customer interactions. An alumna of Harvard Graduate School of Design and Baruch College, I enjoy teaching in my spare time. My specialties are courses in design, creative coding, and hobbyist electronics.
